Ways to make a sandwich

  1. Take two pieces of bread
  2. Lay both slices of bread next to each other
  3. Take a sliced meat of your choice
  4. Lay meat ontop of one of the two slices of bread
  5. Add any condiments you would like
  6. Place second slice of bread ontop of the other
  7. Enjoy!
